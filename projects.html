Our Projects

Successful waste management projects across the Middle East and Africa

Egypt

ECARU has been instrumental in transforming waste management practices across Egypt. Our projects in Egypt include major MRF facilities, landfill operations, and comprehensive waste management systems serving millions of residents.

Key Achievements:

  • Multiple MRF facilities in operation
  • Landfill management and optimization
  • Community engagement programs
  • Training and capacity building

Libya

Our operations in Libya focus on establishing modern waste management infrastructure and improving environmental conditions through sustainable practices.

Project Highlights:

  • Waste collection and processing systems
  • Landfill operations
  • Recycling initiatives
  • Technical support and maintenance

Mekelle Ethiopia

ECARU's project in Mekelle, Ethiopia demonstrates our commitment to sustainable waste management in East Africa, providing comprehensive solutions for the local community.

Services Provided:

  • Waste management system design
  • Facility operation and management
  • Staff training programs
  • Environmental monitoring

Cyprus

Our operations in Cyprus showcase international best practices in waste management, combining innovation with environmental responsibility.

Features:

  • Advanced MRF technology
  • Comprehensive recycling programs
  • Biomass processing facilities
  • Sustainable waste-to-energy solutions
