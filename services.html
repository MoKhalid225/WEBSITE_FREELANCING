Our Services

Comprehensive waste management solutions tailored to your needs

Operate and Manage MRF Facilities

Material Recovery Facilities (MRF) are essential components of modern waste management systems. ECARU provides comprehensive operation and management services for MRF facilities, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.

Our Services Include:

  • Daily operations management
  • Equipment maintenance and repair
  • Staff training and supervision
  • Quality control and monitoring
  • Performance optimization
  • Technical support and consulting

Operation and Managing of Landfills

Proper landfill management is crucial for environmental protection and public health. ECARU offers expert landfill operation and management services following international best practices and environmental standards.

Key Features:

  • Waste placement and compaction
  • Leachate management systems
  • Gas collection and utilization
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Compliance with regulations
  • Site closure and rehabilitation

Operation and Managing of Biomass Facilities

Biomass facilities play a vital role in converting organic waste into renewable energy and valuable products. ECARU specializes in the operation and management of biomass processing facilities.

Our Expertise:

  • Biomass processing operations
  • Energy production management
  • Compost production
  • Quality assurance
  • Efficiency optimization
  • Environmental compliance

Open Dump Management

ECARU provides professional management services for open dump sites, focusing on safety, environmental protection, and gradual improvement of waste handling practices.

Services Provided:

  • Site organization and planning
  • Waste segregation and sorting
  • Safety measures implementation
  • Environmental protection measures
  • Progressive improvement programs
  • Community engagement
