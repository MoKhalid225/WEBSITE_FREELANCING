About Us

Learn more about ECARU and our commitment to sustainable waste management

Who are we?

The Egyptian Company for Solid Waste Recycling "ECARU" is the most innovative & experienced company in the Middle East specialized in solid waste management, landfill, Material Recovery Facility, MRF (operation, upgrade, maintenance and technical support).

With years of expertise and a dedicated team of professionals, we have established ourselves as a leader in sustainable waste management solutions across the region.

Our Approach

At ECARU, we take a comprehensive and sustainable approach to waste management. Our methodology combines cutting-edge technology with proven practices to deliver efficient and environmentally responsible solutions.

Innovation

We continuously invest in the latest technologies and methodologies to improve our services.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is at the core of everything we do.

Partnership

We work closely with communities and stakeholders to achieve shared goals.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in all our operations and services.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable waste management solutions in the Middle East and Africa, setting new standards for environmental stewardship and operational excellence.

We envision a future where waste is transformed into valuable resources, contributing to a circular economy and a cleaner, healthier planet for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative, efficient, and sustainable waste management solutions that protect the environment while creating value for our clients and communities.

  • Deliver world-class waste management services
  • Promote recycling and resource recovery
  • Minimize environmental impact through best practices
  • Support sustainable development goals
  • Foster innovation in waste management technology
  • Build strong partnerships with stakeholders

Training

ECARU is committed to developing the skills and knowledge of our workforce and partners. We offer comprehensive training programs covering all aspects of waste management operations.

Our training initiatives include:

Technical Training

Hands-on training on equipment operation, maintenance, and safety protocols.

Management Training

Leadership and management skills development for supervisory roles.

Safety Training

Comprehensive safety training to ensure a secure working environment.

SAP

ECARU utilizes SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products) enterprise software to streamline our operations, improve efficiency, and ensure optimal resource management.

Our SAP implementation enables us to:

  • Manage operations more effectively
  • Track and analyze performance metrics
  • Optimize resource allocation
  • Enhance decision-making processes
  • Improve customer service delivery
